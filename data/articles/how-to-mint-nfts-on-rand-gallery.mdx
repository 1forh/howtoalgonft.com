## Getting started

First off, you'll need a wallet and some algos. Check out the <Link href="/"><a>homepage</a></Link> for guides on how to get started.

## Sign in to Rand Gallery

When you have a wallet and some algos, go to <a href='https://www.randgallery.com' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer'>Rand Gallery</a>.

![Rand Gallery homepage](/uploads/how-to-mint-nfts-on-rand-gallery/rand-gallery-homepage.png)

Click the `Sign in` link in the top right corner of the page and sign in with your wallet.

![Rand Gallery sign in](/uploads/how-to-mint-nfts-on-rand-gallery/sign-in.png)


## Minting an NFT 

Now that you're signed in, you can mint an NFT. 

Start by clicking your address in the top right corner to go to your profile.

![address in top right corner of page](/uploads/how-to-mint-nfts-on-rand-gallery/top-right-corner.png)

Once you're on your profile, you'll see a row of buttons in the center of the page. One of them is `Mint NFT`. Click this button.

![row of buttons](/uploads/how-to-mint-nfts-on-rand-gallery/actions.png)

A modal will appear with some fields to fill out. When you fill out all of the fields and upload your media file, the message at the bottom will say `Ready to Mint` and you can click the `Mint` button.

![mint an nft](/uploads/how-to-mint-nfts-on-rand-gallery/mint.png)
